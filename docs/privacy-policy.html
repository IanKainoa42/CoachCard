CoachCard — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Overview

CoachCard is designed with your privacy in mind. The app operates entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

Data Collection

CoachCard does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No cookies or advertising identifiers
  • No third-party SDKs, analytics services, or ad networks
  • No account creation or login required

Data Storage

All content you create in CoachCard — cards, scores, and whiteboard drawings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Network Usage

CoachCard makes zero network requests. The app functions entirely offline with no internet connection required.

Children's Privacy

CoachCard does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date.
